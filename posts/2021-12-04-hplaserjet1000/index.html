tgauweiler / HP Laserjet 1000 Constant paper jam

2021-12-04

Introduction

HP Laserjet 1000 always had a paper jam after printing the first page.

Repair

The first try was cleaning the rubber pads for the paperfeed, which collected a fair amount of black toner dust.

Make sure to clip it into the marked clips.

This didn’t solve the problem yet. After some googling I found this video, which sounds really familiar, but for a newer model.

Opening

  1. Remove back cover
  2. Remove screw for side cover
  3. Unclip side cover in the bottom and inside the front

Clean

The actuator hinge was a litte sticky and stuck a little.

On the pad was a lot off sticky toner dust.

Cleaning with sandpaper helped a little, but a strip of tape was needed.

Conclusion

Works so far with the clean job only. But the plastic is really brittle, so a lot of plastic clips broke while disassembly.
